Learn

—
Learn
I am aware of BT Wholesale and want to understand what's available.
Theme: Accurate and easy
Thinking
  • BT is a well-established, safe choice.
  • What new solutions are available to me?
Doing
  • Attending industry conferences and forums.
  • Visiting BT Wholesale website.
Feeling
  • Curious about what's available and how to partner.
Insight
A single second of added page load speed can cause sales to drop. Discoverability and speed of access to relevant information at this stage is critical.

Buy

1
Buy
I become a BT Wholesale customer.
Theme: Easy to set up
Moment that matters
Thinking
  • Hoping it doesn't take long to set up my account.
  • Have I effectively relayed our requirements?
Doing
  • Asking BT Wholesale for an overview of the partnership process.
  • Registering online or via Account Manager.
Feeling
  • Anxious — did I give the right information?
Insight
"Customers who have experienced or are currently experiencing the most friction are those trying to get started." Onboarding is the most cited pain point across the journey.
2
Buy
I want you to recommend and quote a product based on my needs.
Theme: Help is available
Thinking
  • Need more information before making a decision.
  • Conscious of my stakeholders' interests.
Doing
  • Confirming detailed requirements with a Sales Specialist.
  • Comparing options and requesting a formal quote.
Feeling
  • Conscious of their stakeholders' expectations.
Insight
"BTW providing a single quoting tool (drag and drop) where we can quote and order would be a huge improvement." The quoting process is described as labour-intensive with no single tool.
3
Buy
I decide to use BT APIs.
Theme: It meets my needs
Thinking
  • What do we need to get started — is everything in place?
  • Can my systems communicate with BT's systems?
Doing
  • Finding API credentials (previously set up).
  • Setting up testing environment.
Feeling
  • Anxious — will my systems communicate with BT's?
Insight
Customers need API credentials, a test emulator, a test library, and clear documentation — all in one place. Currently scattered across developer.bt.com with poor discoverability.
4
Buy
I'm happy with the quote and I place my order.
Theme: The right solution
Thinking
  • Is this process going to be painfully slow?
  • When can we go live?
Doing
  • Placing the order via portal or API.
  • Confirming documentation is complete.
Feeling
  • Thankful to reach this milestone.
Insight
"The ordering portal is clunky and frustrating to use. It is not intuitive." Order placement is cited as a high-friction, high-effort step.

Get

5
Get
I want to know the status and progress of my order.
Theme: Easy to use
Thinking
  • Does it look like the order is progressing as agreed?
Doing
  • Scrutinising order updates and measuring against expectations.
Feeling
  • Empowered to set appropriate expectations with stakeholders.
Insight
"Eco via the wholesale website is extremely slow and can be difficult to track." Real-time tracking is a minimum expectation, not a nice-to-have.
6
Get
My order is complete and I have everything I'm paying for.
Theme: Delivered as promised
Moment that matters
Thinking
  • Hoping products are activated as planned.
Doing
  • Following confirmation through the portal.
  • Scrutinising that delivery matches the order.
Feeling
  • Relieved — installation successful.
  • Curious about what comes next.
Insight
"Always having to send mail and we spend a lot of time chasing." Order completion confirmation is too manual and slow — customers shouldn't be chasing BT to confirm their own activation.

Use

7
Use
I can independently access and manage my service.
Theme: Easy to use
Thinking
  • I'm busy — I need support within my business.
  • Is my service performing as expected?
Doing
  • Setting and amending access privileges for colleagues.
  • Logging into the portal and navigating to recent activity.
Feeling
  • Empowered to give the right access to their team.
  • Nervous about unexpected fees or overspill.
Insight
User access and role management is a daily job for customers, not an admin task. They need to delegate, restrict, and audit access without calling BT. This directly maps to the RBAC work.

Pay

8
Pay
I'm billed as agreed.
Theme: Accurate and easy
Thinking
  • I have some free time — let me check what's on my next invoice.
Doing
  • Logging in to check the amount due and validate it.
Feeling
  • Nervous about unexpected charges.
Insight
"I want super accurate electronic invoices so I can do reconciliation." Billing accuracy and format (electronic, exportable, structured) is critical for resellers doing their own billing downstream.
9
Pay
I pay my bill.
Theme: Accurate and easy
Thinking
  • What's the latest date I can settle by?
  • What are my payment options?
Doing
  • Checking amount and ensuring funds are available.
  • Paying through the portal or direct debit.
Feeling
  • Relieved — bill is paid, one less thing to think about.

Support

10
Support
I need help with my order.
Theme: Help is available
Thinking
  • Can I make changes to my order?
  • When is the latest date I can change the details?
Doing
  • Checking if there's a cut-off for changes.
  • Contacting BT via phone or chat.
Feeling
  • Anxious that they won't be able to change the date in time.
Insight
"Nobody wants to hunt for a phone number to reach an agent." Support discoverability and self-serve capabilities are low. Contact volumes are high because self-serve fails.
11
Support
I need help with my services.
Theme: Help is available
Thinking
  • I need to understand what is wrong with my service.
Doing
  • Logging into the portal and checking notification feed.
  • Raising a fault ticket.
Feeling
  • Frustrated — a fault is impacting their customers.
Insight
"The ticketing system is so slow and tickets regularly disappear." Fault management is a major pain. Customers lose confidence when tickets vanish or don't update.
12
Support
I need help with my bill.
Theme: Help is available
Thinking
  • I'm worried our bill is not accurate.
  • This is going to take time to correct.
Doing
  • Querying usage with colleagues to verify before calling BT.
  • Raising a billing query.
Feeling
  • Annoyed at having to spend time correcting something avoidable.
Insight
"Incorrect invoices affect the customer directly. We credit it back to our customers, so we eat the cost." Billing errors have downstream financial impact on resellers — more than a portal frustration, it's a business liability.
