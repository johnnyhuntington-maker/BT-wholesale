# Nexus Portal Architecture Workshop Notes

## Introduction

Project Nexus will build a unified digital portal via an API-first platform for BT Wholesale partners, providing a single, coherent way to onboard, buy, manage and support strategic products. The programme will deliver a best-in-class customer experience by simplifying journeys across products, removing fragmentation and enabling customers to self-serve consistently and confidently through one integrated experience.

By standardising and reusing APIs, shared UI components and common journey patterns, Nexus will significantly accelerate how new Wholesale products are brought to market. This foundation will allow strategic products to launch faster, scale more easily and evolve over time, while creating a clear and intuitive upgrade path from legacy services to BT Wholesale's future portfolio. AI capabilities will be adopted where they can enhance the customer experience — improving journey guidance, reducing friction and proactively supporting customers throughout their portal and API journeys.

**Strategic positioning:**
- **Breadth of expertise** — demonstrating BT Wholesale's deep industry knowledge, operational expertise and scale through simple, reliable digital journeys
- **Ultimate reach** — making it easier for partners to access and manage services delivered over the UK's largest network
- **Innovative technology solutions** — enabling faster delivery of innovative, cost-effective products through modern APIs and portals
